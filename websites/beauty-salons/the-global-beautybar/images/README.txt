  break.jpg                1920x800, full-bleed image break between sections

  suites/japanese.jpg      800x1000, portrait — Japanese suite interior

  sfeer/01.jpg .. 08.jpg   720x900 each, atmospheric details
                           (candles, oils, drapes, hot stones, foam, flames)

Tips:
  - Keep palette warm: amber, terracotta, deep red, charcoal
  - Avoid bright daylight — the site assumes evening/night mood
  - Use slight grain or analog warmth for sensual feel
  - No people in atmosphere shots; suite shots can have one figure
    if it serves composition (silhouette, hands only, abstract)
  - Test on the actual site after dropping — backgrounds use cover
    crop, so over-cropping at the top/bottom is fine
