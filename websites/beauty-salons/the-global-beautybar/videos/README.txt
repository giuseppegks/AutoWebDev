Hero video clips
================

Drop 3 atmospheric clips named:

  hero-1.mp4
  hero-2.mp4
  hero-3.mp4

Specs (per clip):
  - Format:   MP4, H.264, AAC stripped (mute) or empty audio
  - Length:   4-8 seconds, looping-friendly
  - Size:     1080p (1920x1080), < 4 MB
  - Style:    NO PEOPLE. Atmosphere only.

Mood references:
  - oil pour into a copper bowl
  - steam drifting across silk
  - candle flame, slow flicker
  - hot stones being rotated
  - black soap whipped to foam
  - drape of velvet falling

Free sources (legal, royalty-free):
  - Pexels videos:   https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/spa/
  - Pixabay videos:  https://pixabay.com/videos/search/spa/
  - Coverr:          https://coverr.co/

Always download originals, not the 720p web previews.

After dropping the files in this folder, the hero rotator
(js/main.js) picks them up automatically — no code change needed.

The poster image is at images/hero-poster.jpg — use a still
from one of the clips so the page doesn't flash black before
the video loads.
